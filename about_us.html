About us

We are a media company established in 2018 and located in Clare, Ireland. Radio is our passion, so we live and we eat radio. Internet radio has become one of the most sought after medium of communication as it is very easy to setup and less expensive when compared to other mediums. Almost all media device are optimized for internet access, and therefore can serve as a medium to revieve online radio. With high speed broadband comes quality content as far as internet radio is concerned.



Radio z1 broadcasts a diverse range of locally and nationally produced programs, both music and spoken word, in hi-fi stereo. Radio z1 broadcasters believe in providing real music variety, so listeners can enjoy a vast catalogue of known and unknown tracks, from Country to Dance, Hip-Hop to Classical, Jazz to Alternative, Rock to Folk, Blues to Ethnic, and much more.
